## Story Information

**Story Type:** Strategic Narrative

**Framework Used:** Pitch Narrative

**Purpose:** Capstone demo pitch to faculty/evaluators; recruit users and partners

**Target Audience:** Instructors, industry reviewers, early employer partners, job seekers

## Story Structure

### Opening Hook

Every day, millions apply for jobs they never hear back from—not because they’re unqualified, but because the system can’t see who they are.

### Core Narrative

We’re building Job Portal, a two-sided platform that connects people and opportunities with speed and clarity. In minutes, a job seeker uploads a resume and gets explainable recommendations—“Why this job?” highlights the exact skills and experiences that fit. Employers post a role and immediately triage a high-signal shortlist in a smart inbox, scheduling interviews without email ping-pong. Under the hood, a hybrid matching engine blends keyword search and semantic embeddings, guided by a growing skills graph and real user feedback. The result is a faster, fairer path from application to interview.

### Key Story Beats

- Problem: Noise, opacity, slow feedback
- Vision: Transparent, explainable matching and efficient workflows
- Proof: Thin, end-to-end MVP slice with measurable metrics
- Action: Pilot with seekers and employers; iterate fast with feedback

### Emotional Arc

Begin with frustration and silence → introduce hope through clarity → build momentum with visible progress → end with confidence and opportunity.

### Resolution/Call to Action

Join our pilot. Bring a job or a resume. Let’s make the first week on the platform the most productive week of your search or your hiring.

## Complete Story

Applications shouldn’t disappear into a void. Job seekers deserve to know why an opportunity fits, and employers deserve an inbox that respects their time. Job Portal makes both possible. We start with a simple truth: trust is built with transparency. Our recommendations tell you why they’re relevant—skills, titles, and experiences that match. Our employer views surface what matters—the strongest signals, cleanly presented. We focus on one thin, powerful slice: onboarding to recommendation to apply to interview. We measure everything, learn fast, and keep humans in the loop. If you’ve ever felt lost in the search, or buried in resumes, this story ends differently: with clarity, momentum, and results.

### Extended Version (Article/Presentation)

The hiring market breaks in two ways: seekers can’t get seen, and employers can’t find signal. Job Portal fixes both with a thin, end-to-end slice that actually moves people forward. We combine keyword and semantic search, normalize skills and titles, and show why a recommendation is relevant. Employers get a high-signal inbox and built-in scheduling. We’ll iterate weekly on real metrics—click-through, apply conversion, time-to-shortlist—guided by user feedback and fairness checks. This is an MVP with a plan: start small, learn fast, scale what works.
