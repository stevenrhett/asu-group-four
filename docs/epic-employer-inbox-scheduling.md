# Epic: Employer Smart Inbox & Scheduling

## Goal
Help employers triage applicants fast, shortlist qualified candidates, and schedule interviews without friction.

## Outcome Metrics
- Time-to-shortlist under 2 days for pilot roles
- Increase interview scheduled rate week-over-week

## Scope
- Smart inbox filters (new/viewed/shortlisted/interview/rejected)
- Candidate view with key signals and resume summary
- Shortlist flow and notes
- ICS invite generation and email integration

## Out of Scope
- Calendar provider OAuth integrations (post-MVP)
